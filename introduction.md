## Teaching Philosophy
I believe that a strong set of fundamentals greatly help in setting up the success of a student. Understanding how everything boils down to their simplest parts will allow one to better appreciate the bigger picture. Afterall, one can only truly appreciate the miracle of seeing when they realize how it’s just our brains interpreting the electrical signals of our eyes as cones and rods get excited by different wavelengths of light.

I am an essentialist at heart, and I wish to teach, to instill not only the main ideas, or concepts of each topic that I teach, but also the most essential idea of life “Love”, because to love life, is to be curious about everything around you, and to be curious in everything, is the first step to truly learn.
